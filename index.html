Alex Scott's blog

Alex has worked as a freelancer in the IT industry for over 20 years. Initially, a front-end web designer, his interest in programming — particularly in functional programming and the Haskell language — has led him to now work on both the front and the backend.

He is currently working on an open source online version of the printed Catalogue Raisonné of the renowned artist William Scott.

Alex is also working as an English teacher, imparting group classes in a fun and productive way. If you would like to know more, you can contact Alex by email at website@alexscott.net.
